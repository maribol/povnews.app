Privacy Policy — POV News

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Summary

POV News is a Chrome extension that runs on your device. We do not operate servers. Your Cursor API key, POV configuration, digests, and feedback stay in chrome.storage.local on your machine.

What leaves your browser

  • api.cursor.com — when you run a digest or pillar suggestion, the extension calls Cursor Cloud Agents with your BYO API key. You can audit runs at cursor.com/agents.
  • Article URLs — when you use “Read in extension”, the extension fetches the page HTML directly from the publisher (no proxy).

What we do not collect

  • No analytics SDK
  • No accounts on our infrastructure
  • No sale of personal data

API keys

You provide your own Cursor API key. Store it only if you trust this extension source. Uninstalling removes extension storage.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the extension publisher listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
